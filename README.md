#  Sales Analysis: Are Discounts Helping or Hurting the Business?

##  Overview

This project analyzes how discounting affects sales and profitability using a retail dataset.

##  Question

**Do discounts help increase profit, or do they harm the business?**

---

##  Key Findings

### Discounts increase sales

* Higher discounts generally lead to higher sales
* Peak sales occur around **50% discount**

### Discounts reduce profit

* Profit decreases as discounts increase
* Discounts above **30% lead to losses**
* At **50% discount**, average profit is around **-310**

### Trade-off

*  More sales
*  Lower profit

---

## Category Insights

* **Technology:** High profit at low discounts, largest losses at high discounts
* **Furniture:** Becomes unprofitable quickly
* **Office Supplies:** More stable but still unprofitable at high discounts

So, impact of discounts varies by category.

---

## Graphs

* Discount vs Profit (scatter plot)
* Average Profit by Discount (bar chart)

---

## Recommendations

* Keep discounts **below 30%**
* Use **category-specific strategies**
* Focus on **profit, not just sales**

---

## Key Takeaway

> Discounts increase sales but can significantly reduce profitability. A balanced strategy is essential.
